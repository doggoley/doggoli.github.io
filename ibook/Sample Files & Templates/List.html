Use the links on this page to connect to useful Web pages for the travel news and information. You can click on any link to connect to it immediately.

Please send addresses (URLs) of sites that you find helpful to ekaiser@hillview.com.

BUSINESS TRAVEL
  • Internet Travel Network's business travel index: excellent list of services and organizations for business travelers.
  • USA Today's business travel news: useful articles and timely news stories for traveling business people.
  • Nerd World business travel index: another fine list of travel resources for business people.
  • International trade: background information and business advice for companies seeking to trade abroad.
  • Association of Business Travellers: membership organization (fee) with a variety of services for frequent travelers.
  • National Business Travelers Association: organization for business-travel professionals.
HEALTH ADVICE
  • Centers for Disease Control (CDC) travel advice: compendium of health-related details for world destinations.
WEATHER
  • Netcast: weather forecasts from National Oceanic and Atmostpheric Administration (NOAA) covering U.S., Canada, and some international cities.
  • MIT weathermap of U.S.: large map shows current weather; click on the map for display of the forecast for that location.
  • Internet Travel Network's weather index: another excellent list of weather information sources.
CURRENCY CONVERSION
  • Nifty currency conversion tool and table of current value for many currencies, with links to related sources of information.
  • The CIA's latest Factbook: detailed information on most countries.
FOCUS ON WOMEN TRAVELERS
  • Women executives' travel resource: useful topics that emphasize women's needs and interests.
GENERAL TRAVEL
  • Internet Travel Network Index Page: comprehensive set of travel topics in a monumental database.
  • Yahoo's index of travel resources: smorgasbord of travel information and services.
