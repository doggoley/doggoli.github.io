Terry A Davis

Terrance Andrew Davis, also known as Terry A Davis, was a programmer born in Las Vegas, Nevada, after a series of manic episodes starting in 1996, in 2004 he began development on what will eventually become known as TempleOS... For over 10 years he polished and perfected his operating system, achieving tasks that were seemingly impossible, all by himself with no outside help. To do something like this is astonishingly difficult, and requires a massive amount of patience and dedication

Terry died last year August 11th, marking this his one year anniversary...

His passion and devotion to this project deserves to be praised, and the operating system itself is probably something that will only be seen once in a lifetime. A brilliant, intelligent, man, absolutely destroyed by mental illness... He may be dead, but his legacy will live on through the people that supported him, and through TempleOS itself.
